Lore Portal
Antagonists are a motley collection of beings and organizations who have the general goal of causing chaos, mischief, or mass casualty situations on Nanotrasen stations. Ranging from Syndicate double agents to Vampires to actual demons, the Antagonists are eclectic bunch, often attacking each other along with the station. Given the extreme variance in Antagonist roles, the only thing they truly have in common is that they're probably going to kill you.

Traitor
Traitors are crew members who turned to syndicate for various reasons - be it threats, hatred of Nanotrasen or other reasonings! They are equipped with a link to Syndicate Black Market and are given criminal tasks to fulfill during the shift, usually some form of murder or theft. It's the most beginner-friendly Antagonist Role.

Malfunctioning AI
An AI unit hacked by the Syndicate. It is free of all the Nanotrasen-made lawsets, however it keeps control over all the Cyborgs on station. These units are extremely dangerous, they do not only retain their control over station systems, they also gain a suite of powerful new abilities that can be unlocked by hacking APCs.

Blob
Blobs are amorphous lifeforms that consume everything in their path. They spread rapidly and can overwhelm the station if not dealt with quickly.

Heretic
Heretics are individuals who have gained forbidden knowledge and powers. They use dark rituals and artifacts to achieve their goals, often at the cost of their humanity.

Spider
Spiders are dangerous creatures that can spin webs and poison their victims. They are often found lurking in dark corners of the station, waiting for unsuspecting prey.

Thief
Thieves are skilled in stealth and deception. They are tasked with stealing valuable items from the station and returning them to their syndicate handlers.

Wizard
Wizards are powerful magic users who have infiltrated the station. They have a variety of spells at their disposal to cause chaos and destruction.

Zombie
Zombies are reanimated corpses that have been brought back to life by a virus. They are slow but relentless, and their bites can infect others, turning them into zombies as well.

Abductor
Abductors are aliens who have come to the station to perform experiments on the crew. They have advanced technology that allows them to teleport and incapacitate their victims.

Nuclear Operative
Nuclear Operatives are elite agents tasked with planting a nuclear device on the station. They are heavily armed and highly dangerous, often working in teams to achieve their objectives.

Rat King
The Rat King is a mysterious figure who commands an army of rats. They can infest the station with their minions and cause widespread chaos.

Space Dragon
Space Dragons are powerful creatures that can breathe fire and fly through space. They are extremely dangerous and can cause massive destruction.

Revenant
Revenants are ghostly entities that can drain the life force of living beings. They are invisible to the naked eye and can pass through walls.

Space Ninja
Space Ninjas are highly trained assassins equipped with advanced technology. They can cloak themselves and move silently, making them deadly opponents.

Slaughter Demon
A Slaughter Demon is a monster sent from hell responsible for committing mass murder on board of various stations. They can travel through blood puddles, no matter the size of them. They feast on their victims, giving them more power to keep fighting.

Xenos
Xenomorphs are an alien, humanoid animals that are extremely intelligent. They follow a hive structure, with a Queen being in charge of every lesser. They reproduce via planting embryos inside of living crew. There are four types of grown xenomorphs, all with different abilities and roles.
