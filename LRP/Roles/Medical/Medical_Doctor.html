Medical Doctor

"No, Medical does not cover soul loss. That's a legal issue. No arm and no leg, though, we can do something about."

The Medical Doctor is the standard role and first line of defense against the station dying - whether that be due to ineptitude or sabotage is usually not their concern so much as what it did to you. You are expected to treat wounds with the provided chemicals and topicals, complete surgeries for more extreme damage and resuscitate or clone dead crew. Any crew who die in a state that can't be fixed should be taken to the morgue for storage. If you don't happen to understand what you're doing, don't be afraid to ask your fellow Doctors or your CMO.

Equipment

All Medical Personnel should be familiar with the equipment they'll need or begin shifts with in order to aid their fellow crewmates.
