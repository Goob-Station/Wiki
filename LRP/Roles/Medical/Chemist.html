The Chemist is like logistics for the Medical department - they supply the grand majority of basic treatments that a Medical Doctor can apply, and even some more advanced ones like cryogenics, pain medication for surgery and advanced topicals. The problem is going to be getting you to focus on that when in actuality you're likely concocting poisons, explosives and ways to mess with the crew so hard they'll demote you to Clown. You're doing it right now, aren't you?

Equipment

A Chemist's gear is simple, yet will see much use. Knowing how to use it quickly and effectively can be the difference in life and death.

Tips

  • There's a lot of ways a Chemist can mix their chems. Some prefer to use jugs only, some prefer to dump everything into the ChemMaster, and some prefer to rely on their dispenser.
  • You have a lot of chemicals you can mix and dispense to Medical. Read the Guidebook, do some research.
  • Liase with the Bartender - they may need some chemicals for drinks - and the Botany department - they WILL need some for mutating plants, and will likely grow you plants to grind in return.
  • Many Chemistry labs have lockdown doors activated internally. Consider finding the button that operates them sooner rather than in the middle of an attack.
  • Even a Chemist can work out in the Medbay - a spray bottle filled with styptic powder and silver sulfaziade can quickly prepare bodies for revival while a water tank with a nozzle can hold all kinds of mixes from acids to flamethrower fuel for defense!
  • CMO may make requests for more medicines - it's recommended to listen to them! If you don't want to, listen to comms and prepare accordingly! Nukies coming? Brute, blood and bleeding medicines will help a lot. Blob or slime attacks? Burn medicines could help there too. Spider attacks? Get something for poison! Is the Captain drunk? Find something that cures it!
