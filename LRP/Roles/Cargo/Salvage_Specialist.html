Salvage Specialist

"Ach, nae body seen ma Jaunter, have ye?" - Hagrim the Dwarf two seconds before running into a chasm, leaving behind a legacy of alcoholism and broken dreams of becoming the best miner around.

The Salvage Specialist is responsible for recovering valuable materials and items from derelict structures and space debris, ensuring the station benefits from salvaged resources.
