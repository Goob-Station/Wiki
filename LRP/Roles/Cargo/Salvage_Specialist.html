Salvage Specialist

"Ach, nae body seen ma Jaunter, have ye?" - Hagrim the Dwarf two seconds before running into a chasm, leaving behind a legacy of alcoholism and broken dreams of becoming the best miner around.

When you are not dealing with hivelords throwing its bits at you, you'll be dealing with everything from crippling loneliness while solo-mining, to not enough loneliness as the changelings cannot get enough of the isolated, walking, DNA snacks that are your fellow salvagers and you. 

Your only means of communication is your beloved standard-issue handheld radio and “vibes”; do you feel like there is danger around the corner?
