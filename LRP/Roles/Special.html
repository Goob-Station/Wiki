Lore Portal
Special Roles are not your typical round roles and are admin-spawned only. What makes them special is the unique equipment and objectives you can get! There's no limit on what you can be, maybe you want to be a fast-talking Trader making credits, or a cream of the crop Response Team keeping the station together and being it's saving grace. They can be a great way of spicing up a round and giving players a chance to explore gameplay and stories that they wouldn't be able to access normally. However, with great power comes great responsibility, this meaning you are expected to uphold higher standards of roleplay and are potentially under more scrutiny.

Nanotrasen Central Command
Nanotrasen Central Command is in charge of Epsilon Eridani, managing stations, deploying ERTs, and handling any administrative duties within the sector. Nanotrasen Navy Officers are often seen representing Central Command to ordinary NT staff.

ERT
Emergency Response Teams are squads of specialized employees sent by Central Command to return a station to a workable condition. This team of Rapid Response Units supports the station in a situation outside of the crew's hands.

SIT
Syndicate Infiltration Team are elite syndicate operatives sent to Nanotrasen holdings to achieve objectives like the kidnapping of command, theft of high-risk items, or murder of a crew member. This admin-spawned role is similar to traitor with the only differences being that you are in a team, given special equipment, and sent for a specific task.

Death Squad
A Death Squad is a team of unknown operators that have one task if they are called upon: to eliminate all threats to Nanotrasen's interests. They are equipped with the best gear and weapons available, and their presence is a sign that the situation is dire.

Trader
Traders are independent merchants who come to the station to trade goods and services with the crew. They have their own ship and can travel between different locations to acquire and sell items. Traders can be a valuable asset to the station, but they can also be a source of trouble if they engage in illegal activities.
