Head Revolutionary

 

You are a Head Revolutionary.

You are tasked with removing all Command From station Via death, Exilement or Imprisonment. The Syndicate has Sponsored you with a manifesto that persuades the crew to your side.

Beware, this won't work on security or command - their loyalty is unwavering.

VIVA LA REVOLUCION!.

 

 

How to convert

To convert other crew to your side you must use your Manifesto by clicking on crew near by to you, do note that there is a short progress bar.

Remember Security and Command start with a mindshield. If A crew member has a mindshield they can not be converted, to bypass this you can use your fast draw implanter to remove their mindshield.
