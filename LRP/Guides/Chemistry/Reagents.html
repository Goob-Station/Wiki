Reagents

Reagents are all substances in Space Station 14, including elements, gases, drinks, narcotics, and other chemicals. This page is a reference page for all reagents in the game.

This page is still under construction. I believe that this will greatly improve the Goob Station Wiki by allowing easy access to the recipes, sources, effects, et cetera, of various reagents without needing to always open the in-game guidebook. However, if the Wiki Admins deem this article unnecessary, I won't take any offence to it being deleted. Thank you for your consideration! :)

Alien blood
Effects

Drink (0.5 units per second)

Satiates thirst at 0.333x the average rate when the metabolizing organ is not a Human organ.

Food (0.5 units per second)

Adds 0.1u of uncooked animal proteins to the solution.

Medicine (0.5 units per second)

Heals 1 Blunt, 1 Slash, 1 Piercing, 0.31 Heat, 0.31 Shock, 0.31 Cold, and 0.32 Caustic when the metabolizing organ is a Bloodsucker organ.
