Crime Categories

These are the list of crime categories and their associated tags. You should refer to this to better understand what a crime is and how it is classified in the list.

The crime times are listed below, and the maximum length for each categoriy is not necessarily the time that should be used for each individual charge of that category.

Discretion should be used when assigning timing of each charge.

- Not all crimes require the maximum charge.

- Not all crimes should have the minimum charge either.

Crime charges, for reference, once more:

- 1-XX: Warning (Note for Future Occurrences)

- 2-XX: 1-2 Minutes

- 3-XX: 2-5 Minutes

- 4-XX: 5-10 Minutes

- 5-XX: Permanent Brig (Bring to Court as Well)

Warnings

Warnings are issued for minor crimes, and should be noted in the event of repeating offenses after the warning has been given.
